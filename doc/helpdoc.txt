This is the Help Doc for the AppAggregator.
If you are experiencing issues with the AppAggregator this is the place to come to.

Introduction
--------------------------------
Welcome to the AppAggregator. The purpose of this web application
is to compare and contrast the apps across the Apple App Store and 
the Google Play Store. Without signing in with an account you will 
be able to search for an app, read the comments of an app, and follow 
the link to the app's store. If you would like to have access to the 
additional features of AppAggregator please create an account by 
clicking the "sign up" button.
